New FOX WWE SmackDown Logo Revealed

SmackDown Live is moving to Fox in October and they have a lot of options. Now it looks like we could be looking at something pretty big changing for the blue brand.

A screenshot from an early Fox promo has revealed that SmackDown will have a totally new logo. It also appears that they have axed the “live.”

Taking out the “live” is very interesting because Fox did say that being live on Fridays is now the “short term” plan for SmackDown.

Superstars featured in the ad include Charlotte Flair, Bayley, Roman Reigns, and Kofi Kingston. So they are pushing the current top singles champions on the brand. This could always change if someone drops the title, but it looks like Roman Reigns and Charlotte Flair are definitely going to be heavily featured because they are the two non-champions on the graphic.
