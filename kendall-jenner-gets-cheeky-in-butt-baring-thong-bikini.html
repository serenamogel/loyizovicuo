Kendall Jenner gets cheeky in butt-baring thong bikini

Bad Bunny is one lucky man.

Kendall Jenner soaked up some sun on Instagram Monday in a cheeky thong bikini from celebrity-loved label Heavy Manners.

The model looked effortlessly cool in her yellow floral two-piece, a rust-colored 818 hat and black sunglasses.

In one of the snaps, Jenner tanned her backside while laying face-down on a lawn chair.

In another pic, the 27-year-old — who has an affinity for butt-baring outfits — looked back at her perky derriére as she turned to lay on her side.

Although Jenner didn’t caption the sultry snaps, her 283 million followers gave her plenty to work with.

“looking, respectfully,” one user commented.

“Respectfully, that a— needs SPF 80,” another person joked. “Hope you remembered your sunblock, UV rays can be very harmful to our skin.”

Wrote a third fan, “I want to be @badbunny ❤️.”

The 818 Tequila founder and the Puerto Rican rapper first sparked romance rumors in February after they were spotted leaving a Beverly Hills restaurant together.

Since then, the “Me Porto Bonito” singer, 29, and the catwalker have tried to keep their romance relatively low-profile, often hiding their faces from paparazzi when out together.

Most recently, the pair went horseback riding together while on a date at the Hidden Hills Equestrian Center in Los Angeles.

In photos obtained by TMZ, the couple were all smiles while snapping selfies as Jenner sat up front, with Bad Bunny holding on tight from behind.

At one point, Jenner — who looked cowgirl-chic in a white crop top, jeans and riding boots with a flannel tied around her waist — got off the steed so that the singer could take a lap on his own.
