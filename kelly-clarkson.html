Kelly Brianne Clarkson, popularly known as Kelly Clarkson is an American who is a famous American TV host, songwriter, and singer by profession. She gained notoriety after winning the inaugural season of American Idol in 2002, earning her a record contract with RCA Records.

She is well-known for her vocal flexibility, having performed in a variety of musical genres, including pop, soul, R&B, rock, and country, as well as for her many versions of other artists’ songs, garnering her the moniker “The Queen of Covers.”
