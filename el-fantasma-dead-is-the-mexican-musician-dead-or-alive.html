El Fantasma Dead: Is The Mexican Musician Dead Or Alive?

El Fantasma is a Mexican singer and songwriter who is known to be singing in the Regional styles of Mexican music. El Fantasma is known in real life as Alexander García and he has bagged a number of accolades.

El Fantasma has released a number of songs and some of them include Palabra de Hombre, El Circo, El Nano, Agarra Tu Camino, Vengo a Aclarar, Los Enjambres, El Rey del Mundo, El Perseverante, among others. El Fantasma was among the finalist at the 2019 edition of the Billboard Latin Music Awards.

El Fantasma Dead: Is The Mexican Musician Dead Or Alive?

The whereabouts of El Fantasma is not known as of now. There are no details and no verifications about the death of El Fantasma although there are rumours circulating that he has passed away some years back. Nevertheless, it is also presumed that he is alive and healthy and has kept a low profile for a number of years.
