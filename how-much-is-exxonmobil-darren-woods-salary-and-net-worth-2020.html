Who is Darren Woods?

Darren W. Woods was born in Wichita, Kansas. He has studied Bachelors in Electrical Engineering from Texas A&M University and MBA from Northwestern University.

Woods has worked at the ExxonMobil company for more than two decades. He joined the company in 1992. After gradual promotion, he has attained the position of chief executive officer and the chairman in 2017.

What is ExxonMobil?

ExxonMobil is a large American multinational oil and gas company. It is the industry leader in almost all the chemical manufacturing and energy businesses. ExxonMobil has evolved from a kerosene marketer to one of the largest publicly trading companies in the world.

Just last year’s reports show that the annual revenue of ExxonMobil is about 256 billion dollars. Recently, due to the coronavirus pandemic and worldwide shutdown, the stock price of the company is getting low.

However, the CEO of the company, Darren Woods says that it is just a momentary lapse and the company will thrive once the pandemic slows down.

There are four brand names of ExxonMobil fuels and lubricants which get marketized globally. Recently, ExxonMobil is working on how to address climate change risks while meeting the growing energy needs of the world.
