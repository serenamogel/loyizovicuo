5 Ways WWE Can Make Chad Gable The Next Big Thing

There are few current homegrown talents in WWE with as much potential as Chad Gable. If you’re not aware, Gable is an Olympian, having participated in the 2012 London Olympics. He signed with WWE in 2013 and within less than a year of his debut, began appearing on live events and eventually weekly NXT television.

He was often described as a WWE Performance Center standout and with his athletic ability, it comes as no surprise. Eventually, after his pairing with Jason Jordan and the formation of American Alpha, people began to compare him to Kurt Angle. Yet, despite all the raw talent that he clearly has, he hasn’t yet reached the level that he should. He’s been floating around in tag team after tag team and seemingly directionless as a singles star.

Yet, every time he’s had an opportunity, he’s hit the ball out of the park, proving just how much potential he truly has. However, the positive is that Gable is only in his early 30s and still has a long way to go. Here’s how WWE can make Chad Gable the next top superstar.
