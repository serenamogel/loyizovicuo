Squirtle &amp; Friends Deck (TCG)

From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia.

The Squirtle & Friends is a Theme Deck from Pokémon Trading Card Game for the Game Boy Color. There are two versions of the Squirtle & Friends Deck. The first one, known in Japanese as (Japanese: ゼニガメとともだちデッキ Zenigame and Friends Deck), is offered to the player by Dr. Mason at the start of the game and is found in the Deck Save machine. It predominantly focuses on , , and type Pokémon. The second one, known in Japanese as (Japanese: ゼニガメとなかまのデッキ Zenigame and Companions Deck), is available from the Auto Deck Machine and predominantly focuses on , , and type Pokémon.
