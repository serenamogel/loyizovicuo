Olga Polizzi Net Worth &amp; Age: How Rich Is British Designer?

What is Olga Polizzi net worth? Continue below to discover the British hotelier and interior designer’s current financial well-being.

Olga Polizzi, Commander of the Order of the British Empire (CBE), is a hotelier and interior designer from the United Kingdom.

She formerly served on Westminster City Council as a Conservative councilor for the Lancaster Gate ward.

Furthermore, Olga has been working with Sir Rocco since 1996 to establish Rocco Forte Hotels, a new luxury hotel firm in which she and her brother are key owners. Also, she is the Deputy Chairman and Director of Design of Rocco Forte Hotels.

Olga is also a member of the Edward VII Hospital Council, the Landau Forte Academies Board, the Trusthouse Charitable Foundation Chairman, the Italian Medical Charity Trustee, the St. Mary’s School Ascot Trustee, and the Lord Forte Foundation Trustee.

In the West Country, Polizzi owns two hotels: Hotel Tresanton in Cornwall and Hotel Endsleigh in Devon.
