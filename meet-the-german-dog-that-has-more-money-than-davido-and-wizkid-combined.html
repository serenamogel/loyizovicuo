Meet The German Dog That Has More Money Than Davido And Wizkid Combined

Davido’s net worth is estimated at $16 million dollars while compatriot Wizkid is said to be worth $12 million (N4. 3 billion) but we guess you have no idea that a dog called Gunther IV is far richer than the two of them combined.

Yes, we are referring to the world’s richest animal which is a German Shepherd from Germany. Gunther IV reportedly inherited his fortune the way many do, from his father, Gunther III. Gunther III, meanwhile, inherited the money from his owner, German countess Karlotta Leibenstein, who died in 1991.
