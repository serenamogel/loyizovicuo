10) Jada Sezer

Nationality: British

Born on March 18, 1989, Jada is a media personality, model, and lifestyle influencer. In 2015, she began her career as a plus-size model.

She has a decent fan following on her social media handle with 296k followers on Instagram. Being a fashion enthusiast, indeed, her Instagram is filled with aesthetic pictures and selfies.

She uses social media platforms to spread body positivity among the youth.

Jada has been on the cover of Vogue Magazine. The graceful and gorgeous Sezer has worked with Adidas, ASOS, Triumph Lingerie, Paige, and more.

Moreover, she is the yogi ambassador at UN Women in the UK. Jada has an estimated net worth of $800,000.

9) Ashley Alexiss

Nationality: American

Ashley is a businessperson, internet celebrity, and model born on November 25, 1990.

Her career as a model started in 2006 when she participated in Miss Teen Massachusetts.

Alexiss is the founder of Alexiss Swimwear. Furthermore, she’s worked with SI Swimsuit and Playboy Magazine.

She also appeared on Fix My Mom along with her mother.

Her presence is significant on social platforms such as Twitter, Instagram, and TikTok.

Currently, she has 2.3 million followers on Instagram.

Moreover, her estimated net worth is $1 million.

8) Denise Bidot

Nationality: American

Born on June 13, 1989, Denise Marie Bidot is a plus-size model. In addition, she is a TV personality, social media influencer, and makeup artist.

Bidot got into acting at the age of 12. Due to her plus-size figure, she faced challenges and started working as a makeup artist.

However, Bidot was discovered by a photographer, and she started her modeling career.

She has worked for well-known brands such as Forever 21, Target, Levi’s, Lane Bryant, etc. Moreover, she was the first plus-size model to walk in the New York Fashion Week.

Having gone through difficulties due to her curvy body, she is actively involved in spreading body positivity.

Furthermore, she launched a movement- “there is no wrong way to be a woman.”

Additionally, her estimated net worth is $800,000.

7) Lexi Placourakis

Nationality: American

Born on September 3, 1988, Lexi is a model, entrepreneur, and social media influencer. She is currently signed with Robert Black Agency, Heffner Management, and Campell Models.

Her career in modeling took its pace when she posted her pictures on her Tumblr.

Lexi struggled with her weight and self-acceptance. She positively transformed her relationship with her body.

She has been open about her weight and acceptance journey, so she has been influencing many others facing the same problem with weight.

Currently, Lexi ranks among the popular and demanded models.

Fashion designers are usually not taught to make plus-size clothing.

6) Kate Wasley

Nationality: Australian

Born on April 16, 1994, Kate is a model, social media influencer, and Instagram star. She started her career on Instagram and now has 357,000+ followers.

She has worked with Lounge, Forever21, Luxe Royale, Avenue The Label, Lorna Jane Activewear, and many more.

Moreover, in 2018, she was featured in the Sports Illustrated Swimsuit Issue.

Kate has been actively involved in spreading body positivity, inspiring thousands of youth to accept their body as it is.

As of 2023, her estimated net worth is $400,000.

5) Jennie Runk

Nationality: American

Jennie Runk, born on November 2, 1988, is a plus-size model and social media influencer.

She debuted in modeling with a beach appearance in H&M’s summer campaign in 2013.

Runk has appeared in magazines such as Glamour, Vogue, Seventeen, etc.

Previously, she was signed to Wilhelmina Models. Currently, Jennie is working with Ford Models.

Furthermore, her net worth is between $1-5 million.

4) Precious Lee

Nationality: American

Precious Lee, born on September 13, 1989, is a stunning and fierce model. She has walked alongside Kendall Jenner and Hailey Bieber on Versace Spring/Summer 2021 runway.

She has worked with well-known brands and designers such as Michael Kors, Prabal Gurung, Savage X Fenty, Moschino, Ralph Lauren, Christian Siriano, and more.

Moreover, Lee is the first black plus-size model to be featured on the cover of Sports Illustrated Swimsuit.

Whatever she wears, she rocks it with her confidence and grace.

Moreover, her net worth is $950,000.

3) Tara Lynn

Nationality: American

The ever-so-stunning Tara Lynn is a model, social media influencer, and Instagram star. She was born on July 26, 1982.

Her career in modeling started in 2009. Lynn has worked with Elle, V Magazine, Glamour, Vogue, etc.

Moreover, she has walked at the Sports Illustrated Swimsuit Party.

Tara’s net worth is around $1 million.

2) Yumi Nu

Nationality: American

The flawless Yumi Nu ranks second in this list of hottest plus-size models. She is a singer, social media influencer, and songwriter.

She was born on September 23, 1996. Yumi started modeling at 16.

She is the first plus-size Asian model to be featured in the Sports Illustrated Swimsuit issue.

Currently, she is signed to Wilhelmina Models.

Coming from an Asian family where women are supposed to look a certain way, Nu believes in breaking boundaries and feeling comfortable in her own body.

As of 2023, Yumi has a net worth of more than $650,000.

1) Ashley Graham

Nationality: American

On number one, we have Ashey Graham. She is a plus-size model, TV presenter, and YouTuber.

Her career in modeling took off at the early age of 12.

She was the first-ever plus-size model to appear on the cover of Vogue. She has been a prominent activist and has been openly sharing her journey as a plus-size model.

Moreover, she has won the Glamour Award for The Body Activist and Shorty Award for Best in Activism.

As of now, she has worked with Bloomingdale’s, Liz, Dressbarn, Swimsuits for All, etc.

Additionally, her net worth is $10 million.
