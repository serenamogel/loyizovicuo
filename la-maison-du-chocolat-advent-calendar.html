The Ultimate Holiday Box of Chocolates: La Maison du Chocolat's Advent Calendar

Remember getting chocolate-filled advent calendars as a child? Well, this one from La Maison du Chocolat is taking that concept to the next level:

Shaped like a Christmas tree, the advent calendar is a holiday decoration unto itself. It houses 24 boxed chocolate "ornaments"—one for every day leading up to Christmas. The treats range from rich truffles to creamy praline chocolates, and if you haven't sampled LMDC before, let me be the first to tell you that they're worth every calorie.

Imagine getting this as a surprise at work from your boyfriend (who would thereafter be referred to as the best boyfriend ever)? Wouldn't it make an adorable centerpiece for a holiday dinner, too?

The 24-piece advent calendar will be available for $62 at La Maison du Chocolat boutiques starting mid-November. Would you rather have a nice bouquet of flowers, or this?
