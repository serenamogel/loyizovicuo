Meet the celebs in Marriage Boot Camp: Reality Stars Season 6

Marriage Boot Camp: Reality Stars Season 6 is here — with an all-new cast as well as a new panel of expert ‘fixers’.

Tonight’s season opener, called Can I Get a Witness?, sees a high-profile gossip blogger reveal a dark secret surrounding one of the couples, while a hearing gets crazy in front of lawyer and Divorce Court star Lynn Toler — and there’s a surprise guest witness at the house.

The new fixers on this season of Marriage Boot Camp: Reality Stars are certified psychiatrist Dr. Ish Major, who previously featured on WE tv’s Ex Isle, and relationship and psychology expert Dr Venus ‘Dr. V’ Nicolino, who was previously a featured doctor on Bravo’s LA Shrinks.

But who are the celebrities on the new season which debuts tonight on WE tv?

Amber and Jim Marchese

Amber and Jim are stars on The Real Housewives of New Jersey, and are hitting the Reality Stars boot camp to try and get their relationship back on track. They’ve struggled to keep their sex lives alive while raising a family and after Amber had treatment for cancer, which caused her libido to drop.

Jade and Tanner Tolbert

The Tanners found love on Bachelor in Paradise but when you meet like that and have your marriage put in the TV spotlight creating the basis for a solid long-term relationship can be tough. They’re coming to boot camp to help them create something strong, and to make sure small problems between them don’t start growing and create a deep gulf.

Karen Gravano and Storm

Mob Wives star Karen is hitting the camp with boyfriend Giovan, known as Storm, to try and get better at talking to each other and make their relationship stronger. Karen, the daughter of mobster Sammy the Bull, is all about loyalty and family but Storm has a history of cheating.

Keke Whyatt and Michael Jamar Ford

Singer-songwriter Keke and husband Michael have become well known to TV fans through R&B Divas: Atlanta. They have eight kids between them but don’t always have the same vision when it comes to raising kids. Both want to become better parents, and to try and put their marriage difficulties behind them.

Margeaux Simms and Merika Palmiste

Margeaux is a star of Love & Hip Hop: Atlanta and is appearing on Marriage Boot Camp: Reality Stars with her androgynous model girlfriend Merika Palmiste. The couple have problems surrounding everything from trust to money and anger-management issues, but want to make it work.

Marriage Boot Camp: Reality Stars airs Fridays at 9/8c on WE tv.
