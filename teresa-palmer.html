Teresa Mary Palmer is an Australian actress, model, film producer, and writer as well. Initially, she took interest in acting line and decided to establish her career as an actress. After landing in the pool of acting gigs, she then developed her interest in other showbiz-related fields.

Palmer is mainly known for her excellent acting prowess as she has given tremendous performances in films including Bedtime Stories (2008), The Sorcerer’s Apprentice (2010), and Take Me Home Tonight (2011).

Career

The actress Teresa Palmer got recognized for her work and wonderful performances. She has acted in the most popular films including Warm Bodies (2013), Lights Out (2016), Hacksaw Ridge (2016), and many more that are worth watching.

Additionally, Palmer worked in ‘Berlin Syndrome'(2017). She also co-wrote and co-produced the drama film named ‘The Ever After’ (2014) wherein she acted alongside her husband Mark Webber.

From 2018 to 2022, the actress Palmer played the role of Diana Bishop in the supernatural drama series named ‘A Discovery of Witches’.

Moreover, Teresa Palmer is also noted for her other acting projects including ‘Deck Dogz’, an Australian film named ‘2:37’, which revolves around a tragic high school suicide. Her well-praised performance led her to earn the Australian Film Insitute Award for Best Lead Actress in 2006.

Achievements

Among various notable achievements, Teresa Palmer has won the Maui Film Festival for the category of Rising Star (shared with Scott Eastwood) in the year 2015.
