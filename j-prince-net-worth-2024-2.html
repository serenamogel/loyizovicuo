Early Life and Career Beginnings

J Prince was born in Houston, Texas, and grew up in a neighborhood known for its high crime rates. Despite the challenges, he managed to steer clear of trouble and focused on building a successful career. He started Rap-A-Lot Records in 1986, which quickly became a prominent label in the hip-hop industry.

Rap-A-Lot Records and Success in the Music Industry

Under J Prince’s leadership, Rap-A-Lot Records signed and nurtured many successful artists, including Geto Boys, Scarface, and Devin the Dude. The label’s success significantly contributed to J Prince’s wealth.

Business Ventures Outside Music
