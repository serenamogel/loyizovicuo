Eddie Money Songs- 15 Biggest Hits of All Time of the rock star

Very few people may have money as part of their name. The mention of money in Eddie Money’s name would probably get you thinking about some multi-million dollar businessman. Yes! He actually had lots of money. But this was through his far-reaching scintillating and exhilarating rock music career.

So how did the money name come about? This will obviously be a modification of his real name, Edward Joseph “Mahoney”.

The Rock star was born on the 21st of March 1949 in New York City. He was described by New York Times as a working-class rocker. Eddie was married to Margo Lee and later Laurie Harris. His wives blessed him with five adorable kids.

The American singer, multi-instrumentalist, and songwriter until his demise on the 13th of September 2019 had a net worth of about $20 million. The singer died at the age of 70.

Money’s songs have continued to thrill lovers of Rock and Pop music all over the world. Let’s take a glance at just 15 of his hit tracks.

Table of Contents

1. Baby Hold On

Baby Hold On is a song about a couple trying hard to stay true to their love promise in turbulent times. Its regarded as one of Money’s successful songs due to its frequent airplay.

Released: 1977

Genre: Rock

Album: Eddie Money

Award: The song reached No.11 on the Billboard Hot 100, No.4 on the Canadian Hot 100, and No.19 on the Kent Music Report.

Lyrics:

Baby hold on to me
Whatever will be, will be
The future is ours to see
So baby hold on to me

2. Two Tickets to Paradise

Two Tickets To Paradise is a song about Eddie’s girlfriend who he plans to take on vacation. It also narrates the selfish desire of some parents who always want their kids to marry lawyers and doctors and not musicians.

Genre: Hard Rock

Album: Eddie Money

We’ve waited so long, waited so long
