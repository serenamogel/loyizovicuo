NJPW New Japan Road Results (11/17): KOPW Title Change

On November 17, New Japan Pro-Wrestling held their New Japan Road event from Yamagata Big Wing in Yamagata, Japan.

In the main event, Taichi defeated SHO to win the NJPW KOPW Title.

The results, courtesy of Fightful, are as follows:

  • Satoshi Kojima, Togi Makabe & Hiroyoshi Tenzan def. Boltin Oleg, Yuto Nakashima & Oskar Leube
  • Toru Yano & Master Wato def. BULLET CLUB (Taiji Ishimori & Gedo)
  • CHAOS (YOSHI-HASHI, Hirooki Goto & YOH) def. United Empire (HENARE, Callum Newman & Great-O-Khan)
  • Yuji Nagata, Minorui Suzuki, Shota Umino & Tiger Mask def. Yota Tsuji, BUSHI, Shingo Takagi & Hiromu Takahashi
  • HOUSE OF TORTURE (EVIL, Dick Togo, Yoshinobu Kanemaru, Yujiro Takahashi) def. Just Five Guys (SANADA, TAKA Michinoku, Yuya Uemura & DOUKI)
  •  Kazuchika Okada, Hiroshi Tanahashi & Tomohiro Ishii (c) def. Ren Narita, Ryusuke Taguchi & Tomoaki Honma
  • Taichi def. SHO (c) to win the title.
